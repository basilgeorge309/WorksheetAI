Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Scribbl ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the app.

What we collect

  • Email address — used to create and identify your account.
  • Worksheets you upload (PDFs or photos) — processed to fill in answers, then automatically deleted after 24 hours.
  • Usage count — the number of worksheets you process each month, used to enforce free and Pro plan limits.

How we use it

  • To provide and operate the service — uploading, processing, and returning your filled-in worksheets.
  • To manage your account, subscription, and monthly usage.
  • We never sell your data to third parties.

Third parties

We share data only with the service providers required to run the app:

  • Supabase — account authentication and secure file/database storage.
  • Anthropic — AI processing of worksheet content to generate answers.
  • RevenueCat — subscription and payment management.
  • Apple / Google — optional sign-in (OAuth) authentication.

Data retention

  • Uploaded worksheets are deleted after 24 hours.
  • Account data (email, usage records) is retained while your account is active and deleted on request.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email us at support@scribbl.app.
